Brand Foundation

Who We Are

Mission

"Help people find genuine,
long-term partners — in person."

What We Do

Togeather Co produces premium in-person matchmaking events — rooftop mixers, curated dinners, and social soirées — for professionals in the Greater Toronto Area. We are not a dating app. We are an experience designed to make meaningful connection feel effortless.

Logo System

Primary Logo

Logo Mark

Two overlapping rings represent two individuals. The centre dot marks the exact point of connection — the moment two people meet.

Wordmark

"To" anchors the name in white or wine. "geather Co" carries the champagne accent, conveying warmth and togetherness.

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the logo mark on all four sides. Never crowd the logo.

Photography & Content

Colour Grading Guide

All photography must follow these grading presets — warm, romantic, editorial. Never cold, oversaturated, or stock-photo in feel.

Preset A

Rooftop Dusk

Deep wine shadows, warm champagne highlights. Golden hour outdoor events.

Lightroom
Temp +10 · Tint +5 · Exp –0.3
Contrast +20 · Highlights –40 · Shadows +30
Preset B

Candlelit Dinner

Warm amber tones, soft creamy highlights. Indoor intimate settings.

Lightroom
Temp +25 · Tint +8 · Exp –0.1
Contrast +15 · Highlights –50 · Shadows +40
Preset C

Blush Social

Soft rose haze on warm neutral base. Graphic and social content.

Lightroom
Temp +5 · Tint +15 · Exp 0
Contrast +10 · Highlights –30 · Shadows +20
Photo Subject Guidelines
  • Candid, genuine moments — never posed
  • Premium venue details (glassware, decor, skylines)
  • Soft bokeh backgrounds, selective focus
  • Warm ambient lighting preferred
What to Avoid
  • Cold / blue-toned images
  • High-saturation or garish filters
  • Stock photo aesthetic
  • Overexposed, washed-out images

Design Tools

Canva Quick-Start

Setting Up Your Brand Kit
  1. Open Canva → Brand Kit (sidebar)
  2. Upload logo SVGs from brand/logos/
  3. Add all brand colours using hex codes (page 6)
  4. Set Playfair Display as heading font
  5. Set Inter as body font
  6. Save kit as "Togeather Co — Brand Kit"
